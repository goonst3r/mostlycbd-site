The ECS Connection

What Happens When Your Body Doesn't Make Enough

RECEPTORS
CB1 (brain & central nervous system)
CB2 (immune system & peripheral tissues)
ENDOGENOUS CANNABINOIDS
Anandamide (AEA)
2-AG (2-Arachidonoylglycerol)
FUNCTION
Regulates mood, anxiety, pain, appetite, memory, emotional processing
Evidence From Research
Post-mortem suicide patients
  • Higher-than-normal CB1 receptor density
  • Brain compensating for low endocannabinoid levels
First-episode psychosis patients
  • Decreased CB2 receptor expression
  • Reduced synthesis enzymes (NAPE, DAGL)
Animal & Antidepressant Studies
  • CB1 knockout mice more susceptible to depression
  • Prozac (fluoxetine) works partly through the ECS
