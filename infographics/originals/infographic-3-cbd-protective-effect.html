CBD: The Biological Brake

Clinical Evidence for Cannabidiol's Protective Role

McGuire et al. (2018)
Clinical Trial
88 schizophrenia patients
1000mg CBD daily vs. placebo (6 weeks)
Add-on therapy to existing antipsychotics
Results
  • Reduced positive psychotic symptoms
  • Improved cognitive function
  • Better overall health ratings
  • Minimal side effects
Key Mechanism
Non-dopaminergic pathway (doesn't block dopamine like traditional antipsychotics)
Morgan & Curran (2008)

Morgan et al. (2012)
Extended Findings
120 cannabis users tracked over time
CBD Protected Against
  • Psychotic-like symptoms
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Memory impairment
  • Anhedonia (inability to feel pleasure)
