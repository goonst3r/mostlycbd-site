What this means for policy & products
A peer-reviewed study of nearly 2,000 real patients shows that CBD dose, THC:CBD ratio, and delivery method all measurably affect anxiety outcomes. The products dominating today's unregulated market — high-THC, low-CBD, inhaled — represent the combination least likely to produce the outcomes patients are seeking. This isn't an argument against cannabis. It's an argument for informed, ratio-conscious, clinically guided access. The data exists. The policy hasn't caught up.
