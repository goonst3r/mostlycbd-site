The Biphasic Curve

Why THC Dose and Balance Matter

LOW DOSE (2.5-5mg)
  • Anxiety reduction
  • Mood improvement
  • Therapeutic benefit
MODERATE DOSE (10-20mg)
  • Peak therapeutic window
  • Minimal adverse effects
  • Individual variance increases
HIGH DOSE (40mg+)
  • Increased anxiety
  • Psychotic-like symptoms
  • Memory impairment
  • Dose-dependent harms

The CBD Factor

WITH CBD PRESENT (balanced):
  • THC's therapeutic window widens
  • Adverse effects reduced
  • Anxiety protection maintained
  • Memory preserved
WITHOUT CBD (modern high-potency):
  • Narrow therapeutic window
  • Increased risk profile
  • Anxiety amplification
  • Cognitive impairment
