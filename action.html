the Objective

We believe in a future where hemp is legal, safe, and accessible to everyone. To get there, we need a unified voice in Washington. We considered building our own tools, but the U.S. Hemp Roundtable is already leading the charge with world-class lobbying efforts. Community means joining the strongest team. By joining the fight at HempSupporter.com, you add your voice to a powerful coalition advocating for hemp-friendly policies. Your support helps fund lobbying efforts, grassroots campaigns, and educational initiatives that are crucial in shaping legislation. Together, we can create a future where hemp is embraced for its countless benefits.

the Feedback Loop
