1 Copy the Script

This pre-written letter urges clear regulation and safety standards. Click to copy it to your clipboard.

Dear [Representative Name], I am a constituent writing to urge you to support the clear legalization and regulation of CBD and hemp-derived products. Current restrictions and lack of clarity prevent safe access to natural alternatives for pain and stress management. We need a regulatory framework that prioritizes: 1. SAFETY: Mandatory lab testing (COAs) for all products. 2. ACCESS: Clear laws protecting consumer access to non-intoxicating cannabinoids. 3. EDUCATION: Transparent labeling standards. Please support legislation that secures the future of hemp as a safe, natural resource for health and wellness. Sincerely, [Your Name] [Your City/State]
